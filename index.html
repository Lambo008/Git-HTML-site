About Chainshopper

Chainshopper is your personal shopper in a click of a button. You can use our service to have items delivered right to your door instantly.
We are an on-demand delivery service for all your needs. We offer a wide range of categories ranging from grocery, pharmacy, gift and many more.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up

There are many ways to sign-up to Chainshopper. Using your email address, Google or Facebook account.

Shop or Send

Select items from our wide ranges of category list or use special order service to shop or send items from your favourite stores, friends or family.

Place Order

All good? Let's click the order button. Before that, make sure your item description, address and payment details are correct. You are ready to go!

Sit back and Relax

That's right. Your rider will arrive with your item very soon. That easy!
