About Chainshopper

Chainshopper is a convenient, affordable and safe shopping service. Hire a private shopper to pick up your shopping & take them to you with the tap of a button on any smartphone device. A nearby shopper will shops for you within 30 minutes. Not only is this an on-demand shopping service, but you can even watch as your driver is en-route to come pick up your order.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Before you can use our service, you need to sign-up with facebook or google account.

Shop

All open restaurants with delivery available to your location will be displayed. You can also make a custom order by swiping the green bar up! How Easy!

Review

The name of the shop is displayed at the top of the screen; the delivery address, items ordered, and special instructions are below. Scroll down to verify charges and payment details.

Place Order

All good? Let's click the order button. It's the green button at the bottom of the screen. Your order should be delivered within 30 minutes.
